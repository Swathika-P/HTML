The Alchemist contains some of the most profound and beautiful quotes on the concept of love. From words of wisdom by the title alchemist himself to Fatima’s heartwarming sentiments to Santiago, Coelho has provided the world with endless inspiring and poetic sayings about the beauty of love. 1. "One is loved because one is loved. No reason is needed for loving." 2. “When we love, we always strive to become better than we are." 3. “Remember that wherever your heart is, there you will find your treasure.”

4. "Maktub. If I am really a part of your dream, you'll come back one day."
5. “So, I love you because the entire universe conspired to help me find you."
